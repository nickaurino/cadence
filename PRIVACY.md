# Cadence — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: June 10, 2026_

Cadence is built around a simple promise: it works entirely on your device.
This policy explains what the app accesses, what it does with it, and what it
does not do.

## The short version

**Cadence collects no data.** Nothing you do in the app is sent to us, stored on
a server, or shared with anyone. There are no accounts, no analytics, no
advertising, and no tracking.

## What Cadence accesses on your device

- **Motion & Fitness (your steps).** Cadence reads your step rate from your
  device's motion sensors to measure your walking or running cadence. This is
  used in real time to match music to your pace and is never stored off your
  device or linked to your identity. Cadence does not access your location.
- **Apple Music.** With your permission, Cadence uses Apple Music to find and
  play songs whose tempo matches your pace. Playback is handled by Apple's own
  Music service under your existing Apple Music subscription. Your use of Apple
  Music is governed by [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/).

Both permissions are requested only when needed, and Cadence keeps working in a
limited way if you decline (you can set your pace manually instead of using
motion, and pace detection works without an Apple Music subscription).

## What stays on your device

Any session information Cadence saves — your current session so it can resume
after you close the app, and your in-app preferences — is stored only on your
device using the operating system's standard local storage. It never leaves your
phone, and removing the app removes it.

## What Cadence does not do

- No data is collected, transmitted, or sold.
- No analytics or crash-tracking services.
- No advertising or advertising identifiers.
- No accounts, sign-ups, or profiles.
- No access to your contacts, photos, location, or microphone.

## Children's privacy

Cadence does not collect data from anyone, including children, and is rated for
general audiences.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new
date above. Because Cadence collects no data, any changes will concern only how
the app accesses your device.
